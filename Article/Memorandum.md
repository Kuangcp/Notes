## 规范
### 提交规范

> github 采用的字符渲染emoji (只能用于github)

- `bug` :x: `:x:`
- `解决bug` :white_check_mark: `:white_check_mark:`

- `待办需求` :pushpin: `:pushpin:`
- `doing` :writing_hand: `:writing_hand:`
- `done` :ok_hand: `:ok_hand:`

- `里程碑` :trophy: `:trophy:`
- `改良的余地` :warning: `:warning:`

> 直接使用emoji字符, 但是兼容性有问题
- [🏆] 里程碑
- [➕]  增加
- [➖]  删除
- [☯️] 修复bug
